the name is the domain

The horse and carriage, rotary phones and even TLDs have evolved into super cars, super phones and, super extensions. Relics such as .net and .com created in the mid-80's are giving way to extensions that now offer precise brandability. Genuine Domains believes the name is the domain, combining to form a complete address solution. Want to make one yours? These domains do enjoy a happy home here, but a moving interest is welcome anytime. Just ask!

about

Genuine Domains is an e-folio of evolved domain names. Made with genuine words and terms, and crafted to span the dot to unleash their full potential. Memorable and familiar, each name reveals its genuined story upon a url visit.
